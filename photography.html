Photography

Photography has always been a passion of mine. It is a way to feel a moment of time without ever being there and to capture memories that we may never get the chance to go back to. Each photo holds its own story, an entire world of events, places, and people in one single shot. Photography also allows me to draw inspiration from the things around me like shapes, colors, and patterns to generate new ideas for my designs.

While living in San Francisco, I was able to capture these beautiful photos of the Bay Area.
