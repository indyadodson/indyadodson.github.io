About me

I'm a senior undergraduate student studying computer science and I love to design. I'm planning on pursuing a master's degree in human computer interaction. The switch from computer science to design was very natural for me. Ever since I was a kid, I loved to draw and create original arts and crafts. In high school, I was a part of a robotics team, where I learned how to program robots.

I decided to pursue Computer Science in college and naturally gravitated towards frontend development. I enjoyed being able to create the visual components of apps and websites. After completing my first internship as a frontend developer, I realized my true passion for design and decided to switch career paths. My background in computer science gives me a deeper understanding of the implementation of a design and allows me to easily validate concepts through prototyping in HTML and CSS.
